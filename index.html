Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why is Healthvalors HMIS the best viable solution for us as healthcare provider?

    Healthvalors HMIS application is the best viable solution for you if you are not using the most advanced hospital management system or web based hospital management system. This ultra-modern hospital management system uses license free software, brings down the total cost of ownership of the product, and improves the overall productivity of the organization by bringing in quality practices.

  • How can I view a demo?

    You can view a demo by scheduling an appointment with us. You can schedule an appointment by sending your request to us on info@Healthvalors.com

  • How much does HMIS application cost?

    Due to varying needs of healthcare providers and high levels of customization, we are not able to list the actual cost of application. But you can contact us to discuss your requirements and to get a detailed quotation for the same.

  • Do you conduct any training session for HMIS application?

    Yes, we provide training to all users as per the defined user role such as: doctors, nurses, executives, lab technicians, billing and finance, HR, etc. Apart from practical in-person training, we also offers help manuals and video tutorials for better understanding and improved efficiency.

  • Is there any online support facility available?

    We have a dedicated team of support executives who can be reached through telephone, remote connection, Skype or online chat. All the complaints raised within application are reviewed and escalated to the support team who will work for you to solve the issue at the earliest.

  • Is there any specific knowledge required for using this HMIS application?

    No, there isn’t any such requirement for using our software / application. As the application is developed in a user-friendly structure, even a novice user can easily operate the system.

  • What kind of hardware installation is required?

    Prior to the installation of HMIS application, you need to make sure that the server, computers, networking, and internet connection are available and operational. The highly experienced hospital infrastructure development team at Healthvalors is available to help you in analyzing your current setup and suggesting new equipment’s installation in a cost effective manner.

  • Can I manage users and their access control?

    The application provides benefits to the administrator, who will be able to manage all users, define their roles and assign required access to the system. It will also provide access to all data and reports such as login log files, MIS reports, and so on.

  • Is the database secure and safe?

    HIMS application keeps regular backup of your database which ensures 100% safety and continuity of your business. Data backup and data recovery, both, are accurate and efficient.
